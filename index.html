The Problem

What is Fake News Detection?

Fake News Detection is the process of using artificial intelligence to automatically identify whether a news article is factual or fabricated. In an era of viral misinformation, automated detection is no longer optional - it's essential.

This system analyses the title and body text of any news article, converting language into mathematical representations via Word2Vec, and classifying it with a Support vector Machine trained on thousands of real and fake articles.

The Process

How Does It Work?

A four-step pipeline that turns raw news text into a clear, confident prediction.

1

You Input the Article

Paste the news headline and body text into our detection tool. No account or sign-up needed.

2

Text Preprocessing

The text is cleaned — stopwords removed, tokens normalised — to prepare it for the model.

3

Word2Vec Embedding

Each word is converted into a dense numerical vector that captures semantic meaning and context.

4

SVM Classification

Our trained Support Vector Machine analyses the vectors and outputs a final verdict: REAL or FAKE.
